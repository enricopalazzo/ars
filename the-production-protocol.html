The Production Protocol

Our production process consists of five stages, with emphasis on fast and transparent communication.

Consultation

After a study of your email or first communication, we’ll arrange a call or meeting to discuss what you desire to achieve with your video, also creative ideas about content, tone, structure, and style. We will prepare a detailed proposal that works within your budget. 

Pre-production

Once the proposal has been signed off between the Studio and the Artist with an agreement form, we’ll assign a dedicated Account Manager Expert who will be your constant monitor throughout the entire process, overseeing the whole project. Depending on the complexity of your project, our team will prepare an specific delivery schedule.

Production

The production materialises at our in-house studio for music and sound editing and specialist motion graphics design. During the production course, you will receive completed compositions via an online link, where you can offer your recommendation directly onto the video for quick and easy communication. Once signed off, you will get the final cut, delivered via secured online transfer.

Storage

We archive all of our projects and footage using a data storage system that will safely store footage for 15 years.
