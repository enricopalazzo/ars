About Us / Who We Are

Since 2020, Digital Performances Studio. Aesthetics Editions.

We are a vanguard group specialised in creating an original and exclusive bespoke cinematographic cut & symphonic/instrumental score composition service for galleries, artists, emerging artists, TV, Documentary Films Companies, and the fine arts market.

Digital Performances Studio develops a wide range of Digital Aura amplifications under a unique perspective of music composers, anthropologists, historians, filmmakers, and digital artists to lead the audience through a specific, intellectual, and powerfully emotional journey of cultural meanings detailed for each author.

In times of immediacy, paying attention is a form of aesthetic experience.

Our Studio is the only one that creates, at the same time, a photo genesis time of exhibition value from the inside out for each work, with a highly aesthetic specialised network of programming and digital configurations.

The Studio works as a creative producer to define and represent the artist's Digital Aura genesis in a cinematic art clip for galleries and the artist's portfolio.

Our essential basis is to create subjective realism and authorial presence in a few seconds, enhancing the objective realism (Digital Aura) with the authorial commentary. We assume the music composition art function as a cinematic narrator, where the artwork elements "tell themselves" to achieve a particular number of desired effects.

Your promotional film deserves to be a unique cinematic art experience.

When we produce content for artists' exhibitions, our goal is to assist the curators and exhibition designers through the narrative dimension of the art exhibition as effectively as possible.
