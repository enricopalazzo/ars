Services

The aesthetic composition service consists of the creation of symphonic Aphorisms, as well as instrumental frameworks and tailor-made sound contexts.

Our compositions exist, to enhance the levels of concentration of the public or audience, as well as the setting in perspective of the work or speech, raising the degree of attention on the time of the exposition

In its first stage, the Studio delivers a product audiovisual or only audio, fully finished with a duration that can range from one (1:00) minute or fives minutes (5:00), attached with the score in digital format of the work, property of the client, subject to copyright and free exhibition for the D P Studio.

Digital Performances Studio has a clear emphasis on the creative possibilities of multi-instrumental sound design, specialising in:

  • Symphonic sound
  • Industrial sound
  • Atmospheric
  • Meditative
  • Rock
  • World music
  • Street music
  • and experimental range sound.
